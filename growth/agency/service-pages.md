your own team running this way, you've already seen it on your own work.

## Service page 2: Consulting

**Eyebrow:** Consulting

**H1:** We turn your dev team into agentic operators.

**Deck:** Your team has AI as a copilot. tsukumo transitions them to running agents as
operators in production, on your existing environment, your standards, your stack. We augment
your developers; we do not replace them. The craft stays theirs, the output gets much bigger.

### The three steps

**1. Strategy.** We assess where your team actually is (not where the demos say it is), find
the highest-impact agent workflows for your codebase, and set a realistic path from copilot
to operator. Honest about what AI won't fix.

**2. Implementation.** We build the agent workflows and the supporting layer (context,
orchestration, observability) on your repo, in production, with your controls intact. Real
work, measured, not a sandbox.

**3. Training.** We bring your developers up to operator level, so the capability stays with
your team when we leave. The goal is independence, not a dependency on us.

### What makes this different

- **Augment, never replace.** Your devs become the operators. This is the honest position and
  the one that actually works: a good dev running good agents beats either alone.
- **Respect the environment.** We transition the team you have, on the stack you have. No
  rip-and-replace, no greenfield fantasy.
- **We're developers.** We've shipped AI to production and felt every failure mode. We
  understand devs because we are devs.

### Who this is for

CTOs at startups and scale-ups whose teams use AI as autocomplete and can feel there's a
larger capability they haven't reached, who want their developers stronger, not sidelined.

### Who this is not for

If you believe AI is easy, that buying licenses equals capability, or that the point is to
ship the same thing cheaper, we're not the right fit. Seats are not capability. This is about
prod-grade and roughly 10x the output of the team you already trust.

**CTA:** Talk to us about your team
