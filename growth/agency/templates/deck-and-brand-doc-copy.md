## Part 2: Design-system doc copy (boilerplate for design-lead)

Text blocks design-lead drops into the brand/design-system document alongside the tokens and
visual specs.

**Brand one-liner**
tsukumo: we run AI in production. A developer studio and AI consultancy that turns dev teams
into agentic operators.

**Name meaning (for the doc's "about the name" section)**
Tsukumo, from 付喪神 (tsukumogami): tools that gain a spirit after long use. The metaphor for
what we do, ordinary dev tools become operators.

**Wordmark**
Always lowercase: `tsukumo`. Never "Tsukumo" mid-sentence, never all-caps except where the
type system sets it. Same house style as `trovex`.

**Voice (one paragraph for the doc)**
Prod-grade, builder-credible, dev-respecting. We're developers talking to developers and the
people who lead them. Plain over clever, specific over grand. We claim what we can prove and
flag what we can't. We never say "replace your devs," and we never use hype words.

**Do**
- Lead with the real problem (copilot→operator gap) in the reader's words.
- Use real numbers (trovex's ~60% per-lookup) and flag everything unverified.
- Keep the consulting position honest: augment, not replace.

**Don't**
- No AI-slop (supercharge, unlock, seamless, revolutionary, "AI-powered").
- No fabricated metrics, logos, testimonials, or client names without consent.
- No Synergix on public surfaces; tsukumo is the only public brand.

**Tagline options (for design to set)**
- we run AI in production
- from copilot to operator
- tools that gain a spirit

**Boilerplate (footer / about block)**
tsukumo is a developer studio and AI consultancy. We run agent fleets in production to ship
our own software, and we transition client teams to do the same, on their stack, their
standards, with their developers as the operators. tsukumo.ch
