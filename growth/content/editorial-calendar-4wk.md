## Standing weekly ritual

- **Mon:** confirm the week's cornerstone or comparison piece; draft it.
- **Within 48h of publish:** fire the repurpose chain (hand drafts to social-lead /
  launch-lead).
- **Fri:** review with analytics-lead — which engine/referrer sent sessions, did installs
  move, did anyone hit the consulting path. Adjust next week from the data, not the plan.
