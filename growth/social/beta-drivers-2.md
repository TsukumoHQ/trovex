> roughly 60% of doc-lookup tokens spent on files they discard. trovex returns the one

> repo that isn't mine.

> else sees it. If that's you — especially a team where the doc-rereading tax shows up across
> several people's agents — request access in the comments. Helping teams run agents well at
> scale is most of what I do anyway; this is the same problem, earlier.
