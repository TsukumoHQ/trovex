# trovex — 2-week short-form social calendar (DRAFT)

> **Status: DRAFT — nothing scheduled, nothing posted. A human fires each item.**
> This repurposes the flagship assets already in `growth/social/` into 14 days of
> platform-native atoms. **One idea per post, tailored per platform — never paste the
> same body to two networks.** Only number anyone may claim is ~60% (or a real receipt).
> Links go in a reply (X) or the first comment (LinkedIn), never the post body.

## Source atoms (what we're repurposing)
- `launch-thread-x.md` — the launch thread + 4 hooks (the spine of week 1).
- `linkedin-1/2/3-*.md` — founder posts (build-in-public, agents-at-scale, receipt).
- `forum-seed-*.md` — the Reddit/Lobsters seeds (fired on their own cadence, see posting-notes).
- The **savings-receipt screenshot** — the single most shareable asset; reused as the visual.

## Cadence rules
- ~1 post/day/platform max. Don't fire X + LinkedIn versions of the same idea the same hour.
- Reply for the first ~30–60 min after each post — that's where reach compounds.
- Week 1 = launch energy (the thread + its atoms). Week 2 = sustain (lessons, proof, utility).
- Forum seeds (Reddit/Lobsters) are NOT on this daily grid — stagger them per
  `forum-seed-posting-notes.md` (build account history first, respect 9:1 / Lobsters <25%).

## Dark-social note (the untracked win)
Most of trovex's real spread will happen in Discord/Slack/DMs you can't see. Optimize for it:
make the **receipt screenshot self-explanatory** (legible, real numbers, one-line caption baked
in) so when a dev drops it in a team channel with zero context, it still lands. Every public
post above should be screenshot-able into a private channel without losing the point.
