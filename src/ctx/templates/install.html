LLM rerank · BYOK optional quality boost

Bring your own OpenAI key. ctx fetches top-20 from vector search and calls gpt-5.4-mini to re-rank into top-5. Improves top-1 reliability for ambiguous queries. ~$0.0017 per query, ~$5/month at 100 queries/day. Key never stored or logged server-side — sent per-request via HTTPS header.
